About

Gudetama is one of Sanrio's more unique characters, introduced in 2013. Unlike the typical cute and cheerful characters Sanrio is known for, Gudetama is an anthropomorphic egg with a lazy and indifferent personality. His name is a combination of the Japanese words "gude" (meaning lazy or lacking energy) and "tamago" (meaning egg), perfectly capturing his essence as a lethargic, unmotivated egg.

Friends

While Gudetama doesn’t have traditional friends like other Sanrio characters, he is sometimes accompanied by Nisetama-san, a smiling, human-like figure dressed in an egg costume who tries (and often fails) to motivate Gudetama to be more active.
