Hello Kitty

  • Birthday: November 1, 1974
  • Gender: Female
  • Species: Cat

About

Hello Kitty, also known as Kitty White, is the most iconic character from Sanrio and one of the most recognized characters worldwide. First introduced in 1974, she quickly became a global phenomenon and a symbol of kawaii (cute) culture. With her simple yet endearing design and heartwarming personality, Hello Kitty has captured the hearts of people of all ages.

Family and Friends

  • My Melody:Her best friend, a sweet and innocent bunny.
  • Keroppi: cheerful frog who loves adventures.
  • Badtz-Maru: A mischievous penguin with a bit of an attitude.
  • Dear Daniel: Hello Kitty’s childhood friend and love interest, who shares many of her interests.
