Kuromi

  • Birthday: October 31st(Halloween)
  • Gender: Female
  • Species: Rabbit

About

Kuromi is a mischievous yet adorable character from Sanrio, first introduced in 2005. She is the playful and rebellious counterpart to My Melody, but despite her tough exterior, she has a surprisingly sweet side. Kuromi's punk-rock aesthetic and sassy attitude have made her a fan favorite, especially among those who enjoy a balance of cute and edgy.

Family and Allies

  • Baku: Kuromi’s loyal sidekick who resembles a purple tapir. Baku is always by Kuromi’s side, often helping her in her schemes, but he’s known to be lazy and loves eating. He secretly has a crush on Kuromi, but she’s unaware of it.
  • The Kuromi 5: A group of minions that follow Kuromi’s lead in causing mischief. Together, they get involved in all kinds of hijinks.
