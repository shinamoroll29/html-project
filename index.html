SANRIO

Sanrio is a Japanese company known for creating a wide variety of iconic and beloved characters, each with its own unique personality and story. These characters often embody cute, whimsical, and heartwarming designs, appealing to fans of all ages worldwide. The most famous Sanrio character is Hello Kitty, but the company's character lineup spans a diverse range, from sweet and innocent to quirky and mischievous.
