Welcome to Sanrio World


Sanrio Characters are a delightful collection of beloved figures created by the Japanese company Sanrio, known for their endearing designs and charming personalities. Sanrio is a Japanese company renowned for creating some of the most beloved and iconic characters in popular culture. Founded in 1960, Sanrio has developed a wide array of charming and whimsical characters that have captivated hearts around the world. Among the most famous are Hello Kitty, My Melody, Keroppi, Cinnamoroll and many others. Each character has its own unique personality and story, contributing to Sanrio's magical world where cuteness and friendship are at the forefront. Over the years, these characters have become more than just mascots; they are cultural icons that inspire a range of products from stationery to plush toys, and even themed cafes and attractions.
