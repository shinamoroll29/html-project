About Kuromi

Meet Kuromi: The Mischievous and Adorable Bad Girl!
From the whimsical world of Sanrio, Kuromi is a character that stands out with her unique blend of mischief and charm. Sporting a signature black jester's hat adorned with a pink skull, she embodies a playful spirit with a hint of rebelliousness. Born on October 31st, Kuromi loves to stir up trouble, but she also has a softer side that endears her to fans around the globe. Often seen with her mischievous smirk, this lovable rogue is not just about her punk-inspired fashion; she has a big heart and a passion for adventure. Whether she's pulling pranks or simply hanging out with her friends, Kuromi proves that being a little bad can be oh-so-good! Join her on her journey of fun, friendship, and a dash of chaos!
