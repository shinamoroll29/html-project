About My Melody

My Melody is one of the beloved characters from Sanrio, the Japanese company known for its cute and charming characters. First introduced in 1975, My Melody is a sweet, rabbit-like character known for her pink hood and friendly demeanor. She lives in a magical forest and is often seen with her signature red hood, which she wears to keep warm. Her character is gentle, kind-hearted, and always ready to lend a helping hand to her friends. With her adorable appearance and lovable personality, My Melody has become a cherished part of Sanrio's lineup, capturing the hearts of fans around the world.
